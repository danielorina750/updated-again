Studio Profile

A multidisciplinary group built for complex delivery.

Established in 2019, SIEN Group Limited is a multidisciplinary design, development and infrastructure consulting firm. The studio brings together architects, engineers, planners, quantity surveyors and construction managers under one coordinated delivery framework.

Our mandate is to streamline regulatory workflows, reduce budget overruns and coordinate high-end builds through integrated design, engineering and project execution leadership.
